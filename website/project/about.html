About Kritikomento

Kritikomento emerged from the mainstreams in the 21st century, born from the collective enthusiasts of a group of artists, writers, and film critcs and reviewers. In a world increasingly sanitized and filtered, we sought to create a space that embraces the cliche, the tropes, and the predictable scenes... and also corporate from relatable, politically aware, socially vigilant stories based on the events

Our purpose is to peel back the layers of artifice in cinema, literature, and art, describe the common yet hard-to-read-and watch of human creativity and imaginations. We believe that true art doesn't shy away from the darkness - it illuminates it.
